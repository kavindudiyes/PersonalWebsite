Goldman Sachs International - Technology Placement

Undertook a six-month work placement programme at one of the leading Investment Banks in the world from April to September 2013. Was a member of the Controllers' Technology team which supports the production, management and posting of financial statements through different applications and infrastructure. Worked on many different tasks and projects ranging from small enhancements and bug fixes to a main project which added an important piece of functionality to an existing application. Gained invaluable financial and technical knowledge while experiencing how technology was used in industry.

Imperial College London - UTA

Worked as an Undergraduate Teaching Assistant at Imperial College London during the third year of my degree. Was selected for this role based on good performance in programming during the first two years of study. The task was to become the Personal Programming Tutor of a group of first year Computing students at the university. It required me to mark assignments and conduct weekly small group tutorials to discuss about assignments, programming in general and answer any questions to help them understand better.

Imperial College London - Group Project

Experienced the consumer-developer relationship by working for the College of Policing (A professional body of the police service in the UK) during the 3rd year group project at Imperial College London. Worked in a group of five where we were asked to create an intelligent system to monitor and analyse crime using resources such as articles from the internet. We used a machine learning technique which classified resources according to "crime tags" with incremental accuracy and populated a database with the information.
